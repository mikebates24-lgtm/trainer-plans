Structure & Rhythm

Week 1: Sweet Spot Build

Target: 200-210W Intervals

Friday Strength (Cyclist Focus)

  • Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 x 10 per leg. Focus on controlled descent.
  • Goblet Squats: 3 x 12. Heavy enough to challenge core stability.
  • Single-Leg Glute Bridges: 3 x 15 per leg. Crucial for hip health.
  • Side Plank / Dead Bug: 3 sets for 45s. Core stability for long days.

Fueling for Speed

Saturday Fueling: Aim for 60-90g carbs per hour during the Key MyWhoosh sessions to adapt your gut for race day speed.
