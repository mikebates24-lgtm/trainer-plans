20‑Minute FTP Test Protocol
  • Goal: Estimate FTP using a 20‑minute maximal effort, with proper warm‑up and pacing.
  • Warm‑Up (20–25 min):
    • 10 min easy Z2 spinning.
    • 3 × 1 min at ~FTP with 1 min easy between.
    • 5 min easy.
    • Optional: 1 × 30s hard (above FTP), 2–3 min easy after.
  • 5‑Minute Blowout Effort:
    • Ride 5 min hard, around 110–120% of your current FTP or “very hard but controlled”.
    • Purpose: open up the system and reduce anaerobic contribution in the 20‑min test.
  • Recovery Before Test:
    • 10 min very easy spinning (Z1–low Z2).
    • Drink, relax shoulders, settle breathing.
  • 20‑Minute Test Pacing:
    • Minutes 0–3: Slightly conservative. Aim for ~95–97% of what you think you can hold for 20 min.
    • Minutes 3–15: Settle into a steady effort. Breathing hard but controlled, RPE ~8/10.
    • Minutes 15–18: If you’ve paced well, gently lift power 2–5%.
    • Minutes 18–20: Empty the tank. No big sprint, just hold or slightly lift to the line.
  • How It Should Feel:
    • First 5 min: “This feels hard, but I can do it.”
    • Middle 10 min: “This is tough, I need to focus to hold it.”
    • Last 5 min: “I’m counting down seconds, but not blowing up completely.”
  • Calculating FTP:
    • Take your average power for the 20‑minute test.
    • Multiply by 0.95 to estimate FTP.
    • Example: 250 W average × 0.95 ≈ 238 W FTP.
  • Post‑Test Notes:
    • Record: average power, average HR, RPE, and how pacing felt.
    • If you faded badly in the last 5 min, you likely started too hard.
    • If you finished with too much left, you likely started too easy.
  • When to Re‑Test:
    • Every 6–8 weeks, or after a clear jump in fitness.
    • In your rolling plan, only adjust targets if FTP changes by ~10% or more.

Fuelling Guide
  • Before Riding:
    • <60 min Z2: optional snack
    • 60–90 min: 20–30g carbs
    • 90+ min / hard: 40–60g carbs
  • During Riding:
    • Z2 <90 min: water only
    • Z2 90+ min: 30–40g carbs/hr
    • Hard/group: 60–90g carbs/hr
  • After Riding:
    • 20–25g protein
    • Carbs if next session <24h
Hydration Guide
  • Daily: 2–2.5L baseline
  • Training: +500ml per hour
  • Electrolytes: warm days or long rides
  • Signs of dehydration: dark urine, headache, elevated HR
