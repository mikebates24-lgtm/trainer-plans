Mike’s 8‑Week Training Plan

Strength & Mobility (20–25 min)
  • Banded Glute Bridge – 12–15 reps
    • Band above knees, push out slightly as you lift.
  • Split Squat – 8–12 reps each leg
    • Rear foot elevated optional.
  • Banded Hamstring Hinge – 12–15 reps
    • Stand on band, hinge at hips.
  • Lateral Band Walks – 10–15 steps each direction
  • Single‑Leg Calf Raise – 12–15 reps each leg
  • Dead Bug – 8–10 per side
  • Side Plank – 20–30s each side
  • Bird Dog – 8–10 per side
  • Banded Pallof Press – 10–12 per side
  • Mobility Finisher:
    • Hip Flexor Stretch – 30–45s each side
    • Thoracic Rotations – 8–10 each side
    • Ankle Mobility Rocks – 10–15 each side
    • Hamstring Flossing – 10–12 each leg
Time‑Crunched Band‑Only Strength (10–12 min)
  • Banded Glute Bridge – 30–40s
  • Lateral Band Walks – 30–40s
  • Banded Hamstring Hinge – 30–40s
  • Banded Pallof Press – 30–40s each side
  • Bird Dog – 30–40s
  • Side Plank – 20–30s each side
  • Optional Mobility:
    • Hip Flexor Stretch – 20–30s each side
    • Thoracic Rotations – 6–8 each side
Fuelling Guide
  • Before Riding:
    • <60 min Z2: optional snack
    • 60–90 min: 20–30g carbs
    • 90+ min / hard: 40–60g carbs
  • During Riding:
    • Z2 <90 min: water only
    • Z2 90+ min: 30–40g carbs/hr
    • Hard/group: 60–90g carbs/hr
  • After Riding:
    • 20–25g protein
    • Carbs if next session <24h
Hydration Guide
  • Daily: 2–2.5L baseline
  • Training: +500ml per hour
  • Electrolytes: warm days or long rides
  • Signs of dehydration: dark urine, headache, elevated HR
