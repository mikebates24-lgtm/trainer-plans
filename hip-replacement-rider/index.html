Caution & red flags
Surgeon & physio first
Not a medical device: This app cannot diagnose, clear you for exercise, or replace professional advice. It only reflects common patterns used in many rehab programmes.
  • Do not start or progress cycling without explicit clearance from your surgeon or physiotherapist.
  • Avoid hip positions that break your specific precautions (often flexion beyond ~90°, crossing legs, or twisting on the operated leg).
  • Seek urgent medical advice if you notice sudden severe pain, a sense of dislocation, marked swelling, fever, or difficulty weight‑bearing.
Reminder: This is a planning aid for cyclists, not a prescription. Your clinical team’s instructions always override this tool.
Listen to pain, protect the joint, and progress patiently – fitness returns, but the implant must last.
