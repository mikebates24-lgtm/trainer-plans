This app does not provide medical advice. It reflects general patterns used in many post‑operative cycling and rehabilitation programmes for educational purposes only.

Any cycling, static‑bike, MyWhoosh workout, or rehab exercise shown here must be undertaken only with the explicit approval and guidance of your surgeon or physiotherapist.

If you have not been cleared for cycling or exercise, or if your team’s advice differs from this tool, follow their instructions and ignore this app.
